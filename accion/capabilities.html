Our Capabilities

Business Intelligence & Analytics.

Over the last several decades, most firms have already automated their transaction systems and hence the real competitive differentiators today come from an organization’s ability to leverage BI/DW technologies. Whether used for operational or management reporting purposes, or for more strategic simulation and/or predictive modeling, BI/DW can be a game changer. However, the diversity of sources of data, lack of consistency of data definition and lack of ownership of data quality has stalled many BI/DW projects.

Business Intelligence And Analytics Capabilities

Accion Labs’ BI/DW team has an experience of delivering over 150+ BI/DW projects in range of industries, functional areas and technology platforms. We can leverage this experience to help organizations realize the value of BI/DW without reinventing the wheel or loosing valuable time.

Business Intelligence And Analytics Capabilities

Accion Labs offers a range of BI/DW consulting & implementation services to develop your reporting and data projects. With a team of BI/DW architects, PMs and consultants trained on industry-leading data warehousing, ETL and BI tools, we offer a balanced mix of strategy, design and implementation expertise.

Business Intelligence And Analytics Services

  • Advisory Services

  • Identifying/defining BI/ Analytics business/project initiative
  • Developing a BI/ Analytics implementation road-map
  • Current state and future state of BI/ Analytics environment
  • Creation of proof of concepts, technology / tool evaluation services
  • Implementation of best-practices and industry standards
  • Implementation of new tools, technologies to provide innovative solutions

  • Execution Services

  • Implementation of all the phases of BI/DW projects – Requirements, Data Modeling, ETL design, implementation
  • Modeling data warehouses using leading methodologies such as Ralph Kimball, Bill Inmon and Data Vault methodologies
  • Implementation techniques using ROLAP, OLAP, MOLAP, etc
  • BI/Analytics solutions using both packaged software and custom development
  • Building analytics tools for industry needs such as: Retail analytics, Marketing analytics, Supply chain optimizations, CRM analytics, etc
  • Statistical analysis tools such as SPSS, SAS, Mathsoft, etc.

highlights of our BI/DW experience
